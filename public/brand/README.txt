BRAND ASSETS
============
Two files drive the branding. Name them EXACTLY as below and the site picks
them up automatically (no code changes):

  public/brand/intro/intro.gif
      -> the looping logo animation shown on the intro / loading screen.
         A GIF loops forever on its own; it keeps playing until the visitor
         clicks anywhere (or the ENTER button) to enter the site.
         Tip: keep it reasonably small (a few MB) so it loads fast.

  public/brand/logo/logo.png
      -> the logo shown in the top corner of every page, replacing the
         "svpermedia portfolio" wordmark. A transparent PNG works best.

Until you add these, the site falls back to an animated text wordmark.
